Bookmark Sorter respects your privacy. This extension operates entirely locally within your browser.

Information Collection

Bookmark Sorter does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Bookmark Data

Bookmark information is used only for:

  • Sorting bookmarks
  • Finding duplicate bookmarks
  • Importing bookmarks
  • Exporting bookmarks
  • Editing bookmark titles

All bookmark processing occurs locally on your device. No bookmark data is transmitted to external servers.

Browser Storage

Bookmark Sorter may locally store:

  • Saved sort preferences
  • Imported bookmark formatting information
  • Extension settings

This data remains stored only within your browser.

Third-Party Services

Bookmark Sorter does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising trackers
  • Remote databases
  • External APIs

Payments

Privacy policy If paid features are introduced in the future, payment processing may be handled by a third-party provider. Bookmark Sorter itself does not store payment information.
